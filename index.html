About Me

I am a high school student who pursues a holistic understanding of the world.

Through a variety of projects and activities including Rowing, K-Environmentalism project, and 2024 Impeachment Photojournalism, I have begun to seek an answer to the question: “How should I understand the world?” However, although I haven’t found a clear answer, invaluable experiences among those projects and activities allowed me to understand that “everything is connected”, a holistic understanding of the world. Here, I want to share my scope and holistic understanding of the world.
